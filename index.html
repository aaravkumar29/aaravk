Admission Procedure

The admission process at RV University SOCSE is designed to be straightforward and transparent.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Applicants must have completed their 10+2 or equivalent with a minimum of 60% in PCM.
  • Applicants must clear the university entrance exam or have a valid score in a recognized national-level entrance exam.

Steps to Apply:

  1. Fill out the online application form available on the official website.
  2. Upload the required documents, including transcripts, ID proof, and passport-size photographs.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Schedule an entrance exam or submit your scores from a recognized exam.
  5. Attend an interview (if applicable).

Important Deadlines:

  • Application Deadline: June 30th
  • Entrance Exam: July 15th
  • Interviews: July 20th - 25th
  • Final Results: August 1st
